# StockOtter Trust Audit — 2026-06-09

Read-only multi-agent audit (8 agents: quant-validator, 2× indicator-auditor, inventory + bug agents).
Grades every strategy, detector, bot, and page as **validated / unproven / broken / failed** with evidence.
Nothing was changed or shipped — this is the ground-truth table.

## Headline

- **Only ONE public thing is properly proven: BBTC's long entry** — clean out-of-sample test
  (+$41k, beats SPY by ~2.6%, fails when inverted so the direction is real). It's a low-win-rate
  (36.5%), big-winners-only trend follower — real edge, lumpy.
- **HTF — the site's lone "green light" — has NEVER been tested SPY-relative on the $5–75 universe.**
  Its "validation" is WFE-only (checks it isn't curve-fit to itself); the only SPY comparison that
  exists was run on a mega-cap basket (wrong universe). The "only validated edge" claim is overstated.
- Two live trust bugs: **Track Record page blank ~1 month** (daily job not writing), and the
  **Trend-Ride bot blends ~18 months of replayed backtest into its "live" P&L / win rate**.
- Three public-surface rule violations: **Insider Trigger** publicly pickable with zero validation,
  **VER SELL** loses money OOS, **KAIROS + HERMES** bot pages reachable by elite (not owner-locked).
